My Approach

Celebratory

At Celestial Atelier, I believe in celebrating cultural diversity and inspiring creativity through the medium of art. My mission is to embrace a wide range of cultural expressions, with a special focus on Bengali culture. This celebratory approach enriches the artistic journey, providing a deeper connection to one's heritage and the broader world.

Expressive

I design my classes to help individuals express their emotions and thoughts, enhancing their ability to communicate and connect with others. Art serves as a powerful tool for personal expression and emotional well-being, fostering a supportive environment where creativity thrives.

Collaborative

I immerse myself in your vision and goals, working closely with you to develop a personalized artistic experience that not only reflects your unique identity but also exceeds your expectations. Collaboration is key to creating art that resonates and inspires.

Iterative

Through an iterative approach, I ensure the final result aligns with your creative vision and reflects your unique brand identity. We’ll work together to refine and fine-tune your artistic projects, leaving no detail overlooked, ensuring your journey with Celestial Atelier is both fulfilling and impactful.

FAQ

How Do I Enroll My Child in Celestial Atelier? +

Starting with an initial consultation, we’ll discuss your child's interests, goals, and how our classes can help them express their creativity and connect with cultural diversity through art. To register for classes, click on the "Register" button to access the registration form.

What Are My Prices? +

The cost for our classes is $100 per month. This fee includes access to all sessions and materials needed for your child's artistic journey. I ensure fair rates and will always keep you informed about any changes.

How Long Does Each Class Last? +

Each class typically lasts about an hour. However, the duration can vary depending on the specific activity or project we are working on. We can establish a more detailed schedule during our initial consultation.

What Materials Does My Child Need? +

All essential materials are provided as part of the monthly fee. If any additional materials are needed for special projects, I will inform you in advance and provide guidance on where to obtain them.

Can My Child Join at Any Time? +

Yes, your child can join Celestial Atelier at any time. Our classes are designed to be flexible and accommodating, allowing your child to start their artistic journey whenever you’re ready.

Does My Child Need Prior Art Experience? +

No prior art experience is necessary. My classes cater to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced young artists. The focus is on expression and creativity, making it a welcoming environment for all children.

What Are the Payment and Cancellation Policies? +

1. Payment: Payments are due at the beginning of each month. You can conveniently pay via electronic transfers using PayPal, Cash App, or Zelle. Detailed instructions for making payments will be provided upon registration to ensure a smooth and secure transaction process.

2. No Refund Policy: Due to the nature of our classes and the materials provided, we have a no-refund policy. Please ensure your child can commit to the classes before registering.

3. Missed Classes: If your child misses a class, a make-up session can be arranged based on availability. However, no refunds will be provided for missed classes.

4. Cancellation: If you wish to cancel your child's enrollment, please provide a notice at least two weeks before the next billing cycle. This allows us to manage class sizes and materials efficiently.
